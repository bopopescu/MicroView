Overview
========

MicroView is an advanced 3D image viewer and analysis platform capable of reading and
writing a wide variety of 2D and 3D image formats.  It is often used for biomedical imaging
tasks and has a specific focus on microCT image analysis.
   
This document describes installation and basic use of MicroView.
